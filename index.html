The Awesome sport of Spikeball

This sport involved spiking, passing, blocking, setting, and a LOT of running and jumping around

The most epic sport of all time.

Spikeball Facts

  1. Skills
    • Must be in good physical condition to run and jump around
    • Cat-like reflexes are a must
    • Hit the ball hard and accurately
  2. Basic Rules
    • You only get 3 touches
    • It MUST hit the net, if it hits the ground or rim that is the end of the play
    • You typically play to 21 points
  3. Pro Rules
    • Serving must be 6 feet away from the rim of the net
    • You are not allowed to "pocket" on serve but can any other time
    • You can only hit the ball twice in a row if the first touch was a block and the ball was still in an upwards trajectory for the second touch
